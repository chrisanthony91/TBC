PROCESS

WORK SMARTER, NOT HARDER

- A lil elbow grease never hurts though-

I put 101% into any project I'm a part of: if my name is attached to it, I want it to be great! I believe that with ample planning, clear vision, and an open mind you can succeed at any creative endeavor; I strive to bring these ideals into every project I work on.

I believe all good things start with a big picture idea so, like any good process, mine starts with having a goal:
What do you want to accomplish? What cool idea do you want to bring to life? What revolutionary gadget do you want to showcase and sell to the world?

Phase I: Research

- Learn All the Things -

Re·search

/rɪ'sɜ:tʃ/
verb

1. Define your users: who are we doing this for?
What does your target audience need, and how can we give it to them?

2. Understand the product/service
Why is our ____ awesome? How can we convince our users?

3. Seek out existing trends and patterns in the market
What does your target audience respond to? How can we do that, but better?

Synonyms: "Figure out how to get the people to want what you've got"
