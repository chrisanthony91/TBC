Christopher Smith

/ˈkrɪstəfɚ/ /ˈsmɪθ/
noun

1. A UI/UX Designer who provides directly applicable, user-driven solutions for digital and tech problems.

2. A jack of-all-trades with a multidisciplinary background in computer science, cognitive science, graphic design, and user experience design.

Some of my interests include snowboarding, video games, weight lifting, adn tattoos. I also have a passion for cartoons and hope to one day become a voice actor for animated media.
